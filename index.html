About

I am a software engineer with a passion for building things that are sustainable and user-focused. I have experience working with JavaScript, React / Redux, Ruby on Rails, Node.js, SQL, and MongoDB, in addition to HTML and CSS. My current project is a single-page app that allows users to search for and review hikes, integrating the MapBox API and AWS S3 storage for an interactive and scalable application. As a former product manager, I love working on cross-functional teams, collaborating with coworkers, and balancing maintainability with moving quickly and producing concrete results.

If I’m not at my keyboard, you’re most likely to find me either exploring the outdoors or trying to expand my horizons with a new recipe in the kitchen.
